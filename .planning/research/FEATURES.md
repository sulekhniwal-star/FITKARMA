# Research Dimension: Features (2025)

## Core Domain Insights: Indian Fitness

### 1. Indian Food Database
- **Open Food Facts (OFF)**: ~10,000+ Indian products as of late 2024. Includes "Green Dot" Vegetarian filtering which is critical for the Indian market.
- **Alternatives**: Bon Happetee (Regional Indian dishes/portions) and Fitterfly (30,000+ verified Indian items).
- **Strategy**: Use OFF for barcode scans; use a local curated "Table Stakes" database for common items like Chapati, Dal, Sabzi to ensure offline speed.

### 2. Ayurvedic Dosha Integration
- **Mechanism**: Use a 20-30 question weighted quiz focusing on Prakriti (innate constitution) and Vikriti (current imbalance).
- **Recommendations**: Personalize based on "Pacifying" logic (e.g., Pitta needs cooling foods).
- **Timeliness**: Integrate recommendations with weather/time of day (Dinacharya).

## Feature Categories

| Category | Table Stakes (Must Have) | Differentiators (Premium/Unique) | Anti-Features (Avoid) |
|----------|--------------------------|----------------------------------|-----------------------|
| **Nutrition** | Calorie/Macro tracking, Indian portion sizes, Water logging. | AI Meal planning (Dosha-aligned), Ayurvedic herb suggestions. | Generic Western recipes only. |
| **Activity** | Step tracking (Pedometer), Streak system. | Yoga & Bollywood workout videos, Localized audio coaching. | Complex gym-only equipment requirements. |
| **Community** | Leaderboards, Group challenges. | Karma Store (Digital rewards/Partners). | High-noise social feeds (Keep it fitness-focused). |

## Dependencies
- **Localization**: Ayurvedic content must be available in at least Hindi/English to reach scale.
- **OCR Quality**: Multi-modal logging depends heavily on 300 DPI images for medical report scraping.
